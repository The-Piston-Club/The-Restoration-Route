The Restoration Route

Your vehicle is tired, scruffy, and in need of a little love, but the good news is that the garages along this route are just the sort of places that enjoy bringing old motors back to their best. Travel from stop to stop, treat yourself to something along the way, and then ask the team if they can help with your next repair. Once you have made a purchase, a member of staff can show you the QR code for that part, letting you restore your vehicle piece by piece. Enjoy the ride, soak up the summer spirit, and remember that a little kindness goes a long way with every garage crew you meet.
